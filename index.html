Who we are

A new foundation, an old promise.

Palliora Care Foundation is a newly registered non-profit organization committed to improving the health, well-being, and quality of life of individuals and communities through compassionate, accessible, and inclusive healthcare.

We believe quality healthcare is a fundamental human right — not a privilege. True well-being goes beyond the clinical: it spans the physical, emotional, psychological, and social. That belief shapes everything we build, from home healthcare and palliative care to rehabilitation, preventive health, mental health support, and community outreach.

We serve people across every stage of life, with particular attention to older adults, children, women, persons with disabilities, transgender and gender-diverse individuals, and other vulnerable and marginalized communities — working to ensure equitable access to care regardless of financial or social circumstance.

As we grow, we're building partnerships with healthcare professionals, hospitals, government agencies, academic institutions, corporate partners, volunteers, and community organizations — working toward sustainable, meaningful impact.

Our Vision

To build a society where every individual has access to compassionate, affordable, and quality healthcare, enabling them to live with dignity, comfort, and hope.

Our Mission
  • Provide accessible healthcare and palliative care services that improve quality of life.
  • Promote preventive healthcare through awareness, education, and community engagement.
  • Support vulnerable and underserved communities with inclusive healthcare and social welfare initiatives.
  • Encourage research, innovation, and professional development in healthcare and social work.
  • Build partnerships that strengthen community health and create lasting social impact.
